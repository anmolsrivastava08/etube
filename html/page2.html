MORE-INFO

ETUBE (website) is the delivery of learning and training through digital resources. Although ETUBE is based on formalized learning, it is provided through electronic devices such as computers, tablets and even cellular phones that are connected to the internet. This makes it easy for users to learn anytime, anywhere, with few, if any, restrictions. Basically, ETUBE is training, learning, or education delivered online through a computer or any other digital device. ETUBE is an online platform developed for the purpose of providing notes and question papers of all year exams.
